# Translation Glossary

Repo: `gustavjung24-ops/ultracartstore`

## Purpose

This glossary locks core English-to-Vietnamese terminology for the project before full localization begins.

Rules:
- Use one approved translation consistently across the site unless a documented exception exists.
- Do not alternate between multiple Vietnamese equivalents casually.
- When a phrase appears in navigation or UI, prefer the shorter approved form.
- When a phrase appears in educational body copy, preserve meaning first, then natural Vietnamese phrasing.

---

## Core Brand / Mission Terms

### Responsible Medicine
**Approved Vietnamese:** Y học có trách nhiệm  
**Notes:** Do not translate literally into awkward or unnatural variants.

### Physicians Committee for Responsible Medicine
**Approved Vietnamese:** Ủy ban Bác sĩ vì Y học có trách nhiệm  
**Short form:** PCRM  
**Notes:** Use the full form in explanatory contexts. Use PCRM where brevity is needed.

### Ethical Science
**Approved Vietnamese:** Khoa học có đạo đức  
**Notes:** Prefer this over overly academic or stiff alternatives.

### Preventive Medicine
**Approved Vietnamese:** Y học dự phòng

## Nutrition Terms

**Approved Vietnamese:** Dinh dưỡng lành mạnh  
**Notes:** Better than “Dinh dưỡng tốt” for public-facing navigation.

### Plant-Based Diet
**Approved Vietnamese:** Chế độ ăn dựa trên thực vật  
**Alternative allowed in body copy:** Chế độ ăn thiên về thực vật  
**Notes:** Keep one main approved UI form.

### Plant-Based
**Approved Vietnamese:** Dựa trên thực vật  
**Notes:** Avoid inconsistent alternation unless context clearly demands otherwise.

### Nutrition Information
**Approved Vietnamese:** Kiến thức dinh dưỡng

### Dietitian
**Approved Vietnamese:** Chuyên gia dinh dưỡng  
**Notes:** Prefer this instead of forcing narrow institutional labels.

### Clinician
**Approved Vietnamese:** Chuyên gia lâm sàng  
**Alternative allowed depending on context:** Bác sĩ lâm sàng / Chuyên gia y tế  
**Notes:** In UI for broad audiences, “Chuyên gia y tế” may be more natural. For professional resources, keep closer to “lâm sàng”.

### For Medical Students
**Approved Vietnamese:** Dành cho sinh viên y khoa

**Approved Vietnamese:** Dành cho nhà khoa học

## Translation Guidance

### Use shorter phrases in UI
Navigation and buttons should be short, stable, and easy to scan.

### Use fuller explanations in body copy
Educational content can be slightly fuller as long as it remains natural.

### Do not improvise brand terminology
Core brand and scientific language must remain locked.

### When in doubt
Prefer:
1. meaning accuracy
2. natural Vietnamese grammar
3. consistency with approved glossary
